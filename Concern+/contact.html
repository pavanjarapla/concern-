frequently asked questions

What does Concern do??

Concern is a health education website , it provides health education to promote health literacy amongst a wide spectrum of users including general public, students, health care professionals and researchers.

How can I join concern?

It’s very easy to join Concern . You can click on sign up , type in a username and password of your choice then sign up. To sign up click here.

What all health information is available on Concern?

The Concern provides information on various topics like Nutrition , Sleep , Fitness , Mental health , vitamins and supplements and Medicine.

Do I have to pay to use Concern?

No. All our services are totally free for you.

Is my information safe on Concern

All your personal information on Concern is safe. We do not share your information with anybody else without your approval.

Concern is available in how many languages??

At present it is only in english language.
